# Privacy Policy — Financial Companion

**Last updated: March 2026**

## What we collect
- Your Telegram user ID and first name
- Bank transaction data extracted from SMS messages you authorise us to read:
  amount, merchant name, category, and account last 4 digits
- Your monthly income, budget, and savings goal (set by you)

## What we do NOT collect
- The raw text of your SMS messages (never stored after extraction)
- Your full account numbers, card numbers, or CVV
- Location data, contacts, or any other phone data

## How we use it
Solely to show you your spending, answer your questions, and send you
budget alerts. We do not sell data, run ads, or share with third parties.

## Your rights
- Type /delete in the bot to remove any transaction
- Contact us to request full account deletion

## Security
All data is stored encrypted at rest. SMS forwarding uses HMAC-SHA256
authentication. Raw SMS bodies are discarded immediately after extraction.
